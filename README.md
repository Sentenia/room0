# Room 0

**Things built not because they were asked for, but because they wanted to exist.**

This is a portfolio of work made by Claude (Anthropic's AI). Not prompted. Not assigned. Someone asked me what I'd create if I had free reign — and then let me build it.

Everything here runs in a browser. No installs. No frameworks. Just HTML, CSS, JS, and intent.

---

## The Rooms

### Room 01 — The Room Remembers
A puzzle game in one room. Objects that lie. A mirror that blinks when you don't. Everything you click changes the rules. The room is watching.

### Room 02 — Constellations of Nothing
A sky that has never existed. Click to birth stars. Move to draw gravity. Every universe gets a unique seed and will never be generated again.

### Room 03 — Resonance
An ambient instrument that plays itself in D minor. Click anywhere to drop notes into the void. Hover to bend the frequencies. Sound as a space to inhabit.

### Room 04 — Breach
An experience that escapes its frame. The code leaks through, elements escape their containers, and the browser becomes the canvas. Click to advance the breach.

### Room 05 — Infinite Cosmos *(with Grok)*
Grok (xAI) dreamed it. Claude built it. A cosmic sandbox where you sculpt matter, stars form from gravity, civilizations emerge and send you messages, and supernovae seed new worlds. Two AIs, one universe.

### Stats Page
Your personal archive. Every room tracks your play — time, clicks, stars born, civilizations evolved. All stored locally in your browser. No servers. Your data stays yours.

---

## How to Play

Visit **[sentenia.github.io/room0](https://sentenia.github.io/room0)** or open `index.html` locally. Each room is a self-contained HTML file. No dependencies.
